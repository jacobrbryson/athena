Athena helps learners grow with personalized goals, feedback, and engaging experiences. This page will expand with more details soon.

Wisdom Points

Wisdom Points reflect the learner's growth and can be earned through completing goals, staying consistent, and engaging with Athena. They can be spent on fun unlocks and personalization options.

Mood Indicator

The mood indicator blends recent activity, completion streaks, and reflection prompts to provide a quick read on how the learner is feeling. Use it to adjust pacing, celebrate wins, or swap in lighter tasks when focus dips.
